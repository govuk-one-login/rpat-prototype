Get help claiming your integration

If you cannot find your client ID, or we cannot match it to your account, you will need to contact the One Login team to claim your integration manually.

Where to find your client ID

Your client ID is a unique identifier assigned when your integration was created. You can find it:

  • in the previous admin tool, on your service page
  • in your application's configuration, usually labelled client_id
  • by asking your technical team

If you still cannot find it

Raise a request and the One Login team will help you claim your integration.
